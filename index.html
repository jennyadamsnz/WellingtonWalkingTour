WHAT

Wellington City is a great place to live and visit and the best way to see it is on foot! So join local Wellingtonian Dave for a unique and colourful 2 hour walking tour around our “coolest little capital”

WHERE

10AM FRI, SAT & SUN at the Tripod at 16 Courtenay Pl.

BRING

- Comfortable walking shoes
- An extra clothing layer just in case of cold or rain
- Cash for coffee & koha
- A sense of adventure!

KOHA

“Koha” is a Maori word for “gift” or “donation” & this is how our walking tour works! We offer our local insider tips & stories & at the end of the tour you have the option to offer a koha that you feel is fair
